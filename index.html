About Us
Welcome to HackSRM! HackSRM is a premium student led national level hackathon held in India that aims to gather the great student minds under one roof and solve potential real world problem through technology under 24 hours. We belong to the technological hub of the country where marvels of innovation and creativity have always been celebrated, HackSRM is one such celebration. HackSRM is a platform for young geniuses to not only exercise their skills to the topmost level but also to build a better tomorrow by ideating, constructing and innovating vast solutions.
Why HackSRM
HackSRM is the perfect blend of imagination, novelty and fun. The 24 hour non-stop technical festival will host an amazing panel of speakers, sponsors and swag ;). With exciting goodies, you are destined to have a mindful of pure genius. If this is your first ever hackathon, we will make sure you have an enriching and memorable experience that you will cherish for days to come.

So join us on this path of epicness!

Schedule

DAY-1

On-site distribution of ID cards and food coupons to the registered teams.

Lunch 12:00PM - 2:00PM

Lunch catered by campus mess.

Hackathon Begins 2:00PM Onwards

Brief introduction about rules and regulations and start brainstorm, implement ideas, code the application.

First Commit 2:00PM - 4:00PM

Hackers should submit their finalised idea and work for the rest of the hackathon.

Refreshments / Hack

Snacks will be served and continue hacking.

DAY-2

On-site distribution of ID cards and food coupons to the registered teams.

Second Commit 5:00AM - 12:00PM

Hackers are required to submit their performed code.

Breakfast 2:00PM 3:00PM

Breakfast will be served by campus mess.

Evaluation 3:00PM - 6:00PM

Inspection by judges and teams are finalized.

Pitch Up 7:00PM

Pitching up ideas and each team is given 5 minutes to present.
