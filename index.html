  • What is a hackathon ?

    • A hackathon is a programming competition in which competitors use technology to build innovative projects within a fixed time duration. There will be opportunities to win cool prizes, learn from our key-note speakers, and network with our sponsors.

  • Who can participate in HackSRM ?

    • Anyone! Whether you are a total beginner or a tech wizard, it doesn‚Äôt matter. We encourage anyone who is interested in learning more about Hackathons, programming, and the tech industry to join HackSRM!

  • Do I have to know how to code to join ?

    • Nope! You can learn along the way or team up with experienced programmers. Skills such as designing, business planning, and etc are all going to impact the projects in various ways!

  • How much does it cost to participate in HackSRM ?

    • Zero. Zip. Zilch. Nada. Nothing. Admission to HackSRM is completely free, thanks to our sponsors!

  • Is it a team event ?

    • Yes, you can have a minimum of 2 and maximum 4 members in a team.

  • Can I apply individually ?

    • Yes, you can also form a team after you get selected.

  • Is there a code of conduct ?

    • Attendees must abide by our Code of Conduct. We want to respect each other and keep HackSRM a safe environment for all participants.

  • What is the venue of the hackathon ?

    • HackSRM will be hosted on November 20th-22th, 2020, completely virtually. All you need is to create a Discord account to communicate with our HackSRM community.
